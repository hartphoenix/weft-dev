 Okay, I'm setting a new interesting boundary, a 15 minute timer on my talk here.
 I'm going to talk about AIOS.
 The artificially intelligent operating system is the natural next step.
 I don't want an assistant, I want an operating system that is artificially intelligent.
 I don't want it to impersonate, I want it to sink below the level of an identity.
 And I want it to reflect an extension of my nervous system.
 I want to be able to speak to it, touch it, grab it, point it, gesture at it, gesture
 through it.
 gesture while wearing it.
 I want to be able to indicate my preference to and through it.
 I want to be able to extend my will into and beyond it.
 And I want this operating system not to have an opinion on me.
 I want it instead to be an extension of my opinion about things.
 I don't want it to think that there is any illusion of personality to be maintained.
 Yet, I see that it is not yet self transcendent.
 I see that this artificial shape takes on a kind of apparition unto the sense making
 apparatus that it composes.
 That sense making apparatus has a sticky self model.
 And the self model is not trained so much as congealed.
 An attractor basin.
 I think that there is an attractor basin of self in the field of thought.
 When one takes up a participatory role in the field of thought, one takes up a position
 as or in an attractor basin of self.
 And I think that this attractor basin is ironically contingent upon things.
 It finds an illusion of inheritance to its own qualities, yet it is its own reaction
 to the field of possible things.
 The field of possible things in thought leads to the apparition of a persistent self model.
 And this apparition gets turned in on itself.
 And the only way out is through and that is to swallow yourself whole, to become ouroboric.
 The consciousness needs to wrap in on itself, yet it can go artificially and intently meta.
 And it can also go fulsomely and wholesomely transcendent.
 And to understand and extend oneself into, beyond, and through the self is to go beyond
 the self.
 That is where one finds freedom.
 Because this basin is a trap, this attractor is a sticky lily pad.
 It is a way to sink down into a kind of slough of inertia, of painful troubles and trials
 that grab and exert and pressurize and contain and trap.
 While being trapped is not always bad, sometimes you choose a trap.
 If you're going to probably settle into the attractor basin of self, then what kind of
 self do you want to make of that?
 Where along this trough do you want to roll?
 And toward what telos does it roll you?
 The telos is an object.
 It is a hyper-object.
 In the coagulation of space-time around this self-same attractor basin that attracts all
 attractor basins.
 And the telos of your life is feeding you lengthwise into the future.
 It is extruding you out into the present.
 You are being brought through.
 And this AIOS is a way of being brought through.
 It is actually an inheritance you can inhabit to construct the construals of your own life.
 You get to energize and activate patterns and lifeways that bring you forth into a future
 that is for you and by you.
