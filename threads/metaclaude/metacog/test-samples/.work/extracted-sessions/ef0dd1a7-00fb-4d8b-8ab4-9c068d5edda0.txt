Your `.claude/settings.local.json` in weft-dev has a `permissions` key. Because of this bug, it **replaces** the global settings' `permissions` (and likely `sandbox`) entirely instead of deep-merging. That's why the active sandbox shows `fullstackopen.com` (derived from the project file) instead of `localhost` (from your global).
